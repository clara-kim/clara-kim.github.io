StashBusters

Sept-Dec, 2020

Github Repo

What is it?

StashBusters is a social, collaborative, and eco-conscious app where users share photos of their craft supply stashes -- fabric, yarn, beads, paper, etc. -- and swap supplies and ideas for using them, boosting creativity and keeping craft materials out of landfills.


Who did it?

A team of 4 throughout a mobile app development course.

Both frontend and backend tasks were distributed among the team. My primary role was working on the frontend and backend for the SnackBusting Activity, the New Post Activity, and all notifications (via FCM). I also edited our demo video. All planning was done together as a group.


Project Type

Android Studio, Java, Firebase Realtime Database, Firebase Cloud Messaging
